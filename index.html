About

Hello! I am a Research Scientist at AIM Intelligence, working on multimodal safety, responsible AI, and large language models (LLMs). I am also an M.S. student at Yonsei University and an active researcher at HAE-RAE, an open-source research community focused on Korean NLP.

My research focuses on building safe, aligned, and trustworthy foundation models that can interact with humans and real-world multimodal data. I am particularly interested in the intersection of AI safety, multimodal alignment, and human-centered, responsible AI systems.
